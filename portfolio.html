About

I'm the designer who reads the changelog.

I care about the 2am user — the one who's tired, distracted, and just needs it to work. That's who I design for. I believe the best UX is invisible: it never makes you feel dumb, never makes you hunt for what you need.

With a background in Systems Design Engineering from uWaterloo, I approach problems structurally before I ever open Figma. I'm most at home in complex, data-heavy products — security tooling, enterprise software, anything where clarity is genuinely hard to achieve.

Right now I'm designing at Arctic Wolf, where I work on the tools analysts use to detect, investigate, and respond to cyberattacks. Every decision I make could affect how fast a real threat gets contained.
